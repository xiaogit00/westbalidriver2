About This Tour

Mount Bromo is one of Indonesia's most iconic sights — an active volcano rising from a vast grey "Sea of Sand" in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park. At sunrise, with clouds rolling in below the viewpoint and orange light spilling across the caldera, it looks more like a painting than reality.

Getting here from Bali requires crossing to East Java, driving through mountain terrain, and timing your arrival to hit the 4am Penanjakan viewpoint before the crowds. It's a journey, but with your own private driver every detail is handled. No buses, no strangers, no waiting — just your group and the road.

This 3-day trip is our most ambitious package, and regularly rated by guests as the highlight of their entire trip to Indonesia. We'll make sure you see it at its very best.

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — 12:00: Hotel Pickup in Bali

Your driver collects you from your Bali hotel. We head east, crossing to Java via the Bali Strait ferry at Gilimanuk and driving toward Probolinggo.

Day 1 — ~21:00: Arrive at Cemoro Lawang (Hotel)

Check in to a cosy guesthouse right on the crater's edge. At this altitude (~2,200m) nights are cold — blankets provided. Rest up, it's an early one tomorrow.

Day 2 — 03:30: Drive to Penanjakan Viewpoint

Wrapped in layers, we drive in darkness up to the famous viewpoint. Find a spot, pour a hot coffee from our thermos, and wait for the world to turn gold.

Day 2 — 05:30: Sunrise Over the Caldera

The sun rises behind Semeru (Java's highest volcano), flooding the crater with amber light. Bromo puffs smoke below you. One of the most beautiful sunrises on Earth.

Day 2 — 07:30: 4WD Jeep Across the Sea of Sand

Descend by jeep across the extraordinary ash plain. The ground is lunar-grey, the sky huge, and the volcano is right ahead of you.

Day 2 — 08:30: Bromo Crater Rim Hike

Climb the 243 steps to the rim and peer into the smoking, roaring crater. Standing here with the smell of sulfur and the rumble underfoot is utterly primal.

Day 2 — 11:00: Teletubbies Hill & Savanna

A surreal contrast to the barren caldera: rolling green hills and wide open savanna, shared with grazing horses and local Tenggerese families.

Day 2 — 14:00: Rest & Explore Cemoro Lawang

Return to the guesthouse for lunch and a well-earned rest. Explore the village, warm up, and enjoy the mountain air.

Day 3 — 09:00: Depart for Bali

Morning departure, scenic drive back across East Java and the strait. Drop-off at your Bali hotel by early evening.
