About This Tour

Ubud is the soul of Bali — and most visitors only scratch the surface. With your own private driver who knows every back road and local haunt, you'll discover a side of Ubud that tour buses never reach.

We'll weave through emerald rice paddies still worked by hand, explore ancient water temples, wander through the sacred Monkey Forest, and browse the Ubud Art Market at your own pace. Along the way your driver will share stories, traditions and insider knowledge that no guidebook can give you.

This is a fully flexible, full-day trip. We customise the route to suit your interests — art lover, foodie, nature enthusiast, or all three? Just let us know.

Sample Itinerary

08:00 — Hotel Pickup

Your driver arrives at your accommodation. Enjoy a quick briefing and any last-minute customisations to the day's route.

09:00 — Tegallalang Rice Terraces

Walk the iconic stepped rice terraces, learn about the traditional Subak irrigation system and grab some incredible photos above the jungle valley below.

10:30 — Coffee Plantation & Luwak Coffee

Visit a local plantation to see how Bali's famous kopi is grown and processed. Tasting included.

11:30 — Tirta Empul Temple

One of Bali's most sacred sites. Watch (or join) the purification ritual at the natural spring pools, framed by ancient stone shrines.

13:00 — Lunch at a Local Warung

Your driver will take you somewhere real — no tourist traps. Think fresh nasi campur, satay and cold young coconut for under $5.

14:30 — Sacred Monkey Forest

Wander through ancient jungle temples inhabited by over 1,000 long-tailed macaques. Keep your sunglasses on your face, not your head!

16:00 — Ubud Art Market & Royal Palace

Browse handmade textiles, woodcarvings and silver jewellery at the market, then admire the gates of Ubud's royal palace across the road.

17:30 — Return to Hotel

We'll drop you back at your accommodation, or extend the day for a sunset stop — just let us know.
